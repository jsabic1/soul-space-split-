Questions

Frequently asked questions

How do I book a session?

The easiest way is to tap the "Book a session" button anywhere on the site, and WhatsApp opens with a pre-written message. You can also message me on Instagram or send an email. I reply within 24 hours and we arrange your session.

I don't know which ritual to choose. What should I do?

You don't have to know, that's my job. Write me a few lines about how you feel and what's bothering you, and I'll suggest what your body needs most right now. Every ritual is adapted to you anyway, not to a formula.

How should I prepare for my visit?

Almost nothing special. The only small preparation is a warm shower before you arrive, your skin receives the oil and touch most beautifully then, and your body relaxes faster. Everything else we agree on in person, at your pace. And if you arrive a few minutes early, you'll have time to land from your day before we begin.

Is the massage painful?

No. The pressure is firm when your body asks for it, but never harsh, I work at a tempo your nervous system can follow. If your body isn't ready to open an area, we don't go there. You always have the final word.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

Message me as soon as you can, at least 12 hours before your session, and we'll happily arrange a new one. Cancellations within 12 hours are charged 50 % of the ritual price, and a no-show without notice is charged in full. More in the cancellation policy.

Where is the studio?

At Sinjska 5, 21000 Split, tapping the address opens Google Maps.

How long does a ritual last?

Depending on the ritual, between 60 and 90 minutes. The exact duration is listed with each ritual on the Rituals page. Leave yourself a little space afterwards, it's best not to rush straight on.

Have a question that isn't answered here? Feel free to write to me.
