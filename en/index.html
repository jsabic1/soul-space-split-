Approach

Not fast. Not forced.

01

System before symptoms

A tight muscle never lets go by force. It lets go when the system feels safe, so we begin with calming.

02

Tempo over technique

Rhythm and predictability send your body a message of safety. Technique comes only after that.

03

The body leads

If an area isn't ready to open, we don't go there. You have the final word, always.

How it works

From message to table

  1. You write to me

    An email or a message with a few lines about how you feel.

  2. I suggest

    The ritual that would suit you best, and 2–3 available times.

  3. You arrive

    The studio at Sinjska 5. One hour just for you, nothing is required of you.

  4. You continue

    You come back as needed. Most clients visit once a month.
